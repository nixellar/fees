    Frequently asked questions

    How to pay transaction(s) fee?
    We use the funds collected from our users to pay for network transaction charges, since every action on a blockchain consumes some kind of "RAM" or "GAS" resource.

    What does Nixellar use the collected fee for?
    We also use these funds to keep the Stellar blockchain updated with the number of Nixellars in circulation and the number of accounts trustlined to Nixellar.

    Who pays for Nixellar's operating cost?
    These funds will also be used to pay for our federation, anchor service and NIX node server costs from time to time.

    How can we support the Nixellar♾ project?
    Anyone can support our project by virtue of utilising our services and asking more friends\patrons to do so.
    Additionally, if one likes to further support us, they may donate NIX, Nixellar or Stellar Lumens to us.
